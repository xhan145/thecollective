Collective Website Review & Recommendations
Overall Assessment
The site is much stronger than a typical pre-seed landing page. The branding is cohesive and aligns
well with the Collective vision of practice, proof, feedback, trust, and contribution. The next
improvements should focus less on aesthetics and more on clarity, credibility, and conversion.
1. Hero Section
Replace the text-heavy hero with a split layout: logo, headline, short description, CTA on the left,
and an animated phone mockup on the right showing the product in action.
2. Simplify the Value Proposition
Use a concise headline such as 'Build real confidence through practice—not likes.' Follow it with a
clear explanation of what users actually do.
3. Add Social Proof
Display beta metrics, testimonials, or usage statistics to build credibility.
4. Animated Product Flow
Show Discover → Practice → Upload Proof → Receive Feedback → Build Trust → Contribute in a
simple animation.
5. Product Screenshots
Add screenshots for Practice, Upload Proof, Receive Feedback, and Trust Profile.
6. Explain the Mission
Include a memorable comparison: 'Social media rewards attention. Collective rewards progress.'
7. Highlight AI
Dedicate a section explaining AI-assisted practice, reflection, feedback summaries, and
preparation.
8. Practice Examples
Show concrete examples like interview practice, introductions, presentations, negotiations, and
leadership exercises.
9. Explain Trust
Visually explain how trust is earned through consistent practice, proof, useful feedback, and
contribution.
10. Exclusive Beta
Frame the beta as an invitation to help build the future of the platform.
11. Founder Story
Include a short founder story explaining why Collective exists.
12. CTA Hierarchy
Primary CTA: Join Closed Beta. Secondary CTA: Watch a 90-Second Demo.
13. Testimonials
Feature early user quotes as soon as available.

14. Vision
Show future directions such as Communication, Leadership, Career, Business, Relationships, and
Fitness.
15. Footer
Expand with Mission, Privacy, Terms, Roadmap, Contact, Socials, and Founder.
16. Mobile Polish
Increase spacing, padding, typography scale, and section separation for a more premium feel.
17. Motion
Add subtle animations including logo glow, card transitions, button hover effects, and progress
animations.
18. Most Important Addition
Create a dedicated section explaining that Collective rewards progress rather than attention and
builds reputation through meaningful practice and contribution.
Priority Roadmap
1. Show the product visually with screenshots and mockups.
2. Simplify the messaging.
3. Add credibility through testimonials and metrics.
4. Visualize the Practice → Proof → Feedback → Trust loop.
5. Refine spacing, typography, and motion for a premium experience.
